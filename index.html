FAQs

Answer 1: The square root of 144 is a number that, when multiplied by itself, equals 144. In this case, the square root is 12 because 12 x 12 = 144.

Answer 2: Newton's first law of motion, often called the law of inertia, states that an object at rest will remain at rest, and an object in motion will stay in motion unless acted upon by an external force. In other words, an object will maintain its state of motion unless something causes it to change.

Answer 2: The Urdu translation of the word "book" is "کتاب" (kitab). This term is commonly used in Urdu to refer to a book or written publication.

Answer 4: A simile is a figure of speech used in literature to make a comparison between two unlike things, often using the words "like" or "as." For example, the phrase "Her smile was as bright as the sun" is a simile because it compares the brightness of the smile to the brightness of the sun.

Answer 5: World War II ended in 1945. The war concluded in Europe on May 8, 1945, when Germany surrendered, and it ended in the Pacific on September 2, 1945, after Japan surrendered.

Answer 6: A chemical reaction is a process in which one or more substances (reactants) undergo a transformation to produce one or more different substances (products). Chemical reactions are typically represented using chemical equations. For example, the combustion of methane can be represented as:
CH₄ (methane) + 2O₂ (oxygen) → CO₂ (carbon dioxide) + 2H₂O (water)
